Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 10, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you:

  • Create an account (name, email, password)
  • Report issues (location data, photos, descriptions)
  • Interact with the platform (comments, votes)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Process and complete issue reports
  • Send you notifications about your issues
  • Calculate and display sustainability impact metrics
  • Prevent fraud and abuse

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information:

  • With municipal authorities to resolve reported issues
  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect the rights and safety of our users

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information, including encryption, secure authentication, and regular security audits.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account
  • Opt-out of email notifications

6. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@urbanpulse.local

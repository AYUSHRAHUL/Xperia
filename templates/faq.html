Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Urban Pulse.

How do I report an issue?

Simply sign up as a citizen, go to your dashboard, and click on "Report Issue". You can use your current location or pin it on the map, describe the problem, and upload a photo.

How do I earn points?

You earn points for every valid issue you report (10 points) and extra points when you include a photo (+5 points). You also get bonus points (20 points) when an issue you reported is resolved!

What happens after I report an issue?

Your report is verified by an admin. Once verified, it is assigned to a field worker. You will receive notifications at every step: Verified -> Assigned -> In Progress -> Resolved.

How is impact calculated?

We estimate the environmental impact (water saved, CO2 reduced, waste removed) based on the category of the issue and standard sustainability metrics aligned with UN SDGs.

Is this service free?

Yes! Urban Pulse is a free platform dedicated to making our cities smarter and more sustainable through community collaboration.
